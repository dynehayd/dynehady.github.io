Data Management

Data Management is the execution of structure, practices and actions that manage and deal with the data of a company. Company’s large or small, in new zealand or in scotland will have to deal with issues that arise with data management, be it updating customer transactions or keeping an up to data inventory. Data that a company must deal with can be of a very large quantity, this can cause problems, NG data sums up: “Data management is an administrative process that includes acquiring, validating, storing, protecting, and processing required data to ensure the accessibility, reliability, and timeliness of the data for its users.” – (June 9, 2017, Molly Galetto, ngdata what is data management?, https://www.ngdata.com/what-is-data-management/). Obtaining data is easy for a company processing it can be difficult. In order to maximise the usage of this data a business must have a up to date and cutting edge data management systems . “The benefits of data management include enhanced compliance, greater security, improved sales and marketing strategies, better product classification, and improved data governance to reduce organizational risk. ”-( © 2017 Informatica, informatica data management, https://www.informatica.com/services-and-training/glossary-of-terms/data-management-definition.html#fbid=rw9OMoXbIxv ).
