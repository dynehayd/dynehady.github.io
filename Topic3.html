Mobile IS

Mobile Information Systems include the communication between mobile phones and other wireless devices. 3G/4G is a must to enable data on cellular networks, Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. These leaps in computer engineering let us share copy and distribute data with each other and the world. IGI global: “Mobile information systems are systems that rely on wireless communications and support mobile applications that typically run on wireless devices such as smartphones and mobile phones”( Copyright © 1988-2017, IGI Global, What is Mobile Information Systems, https://www.igi-global.com/dictionary/mobile-information-systems/18871 ). An appropriate usage of mobile information systems from a business standpoint is that as a business you can create an app in order to sell your produce, apps are relatively low cost and require almost nothing to be distributed. For a business this is substantial because they’re saving money and increasing efficiency. A problem with mobile information systems is that they are much more penetrable and therefore much more likely to be subject to attacks. Mobile information systems have been a game changer in today’s world, granting businesses to connect with consumers anytime and anywhere. Ensuing a large growth in the economy for application based industry.
